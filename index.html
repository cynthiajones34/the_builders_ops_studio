What I Offer

Services designed for real businesses.

Whether you're setting up from scratch or cleaning up what you've already built, these engagements are designed to give you a clear path forward, not to overwhelm.

01

Operations Audit & Roadmap

A full diagnostic of your business operations including tools, workflows, team structure, and communications with a prioritized roadmap for what to fix first and how to do it.

02

Systems & Process Design

Custom SOPs, workflow documentation, and tool configuration that replace the improvised chaos with repeatable, teachable systems your whole team can follow.

03

Customer Experience Setup

Design your client-facing experience from first touchpoint to ongoing relationship including onboarding flows, communication templates, support tiers, and retention strategy.

04

Team Building & Hiring

Ready to bring someone on? I help you define the role, build the hiring process, create onboarding documentation, and set performance expectations from day one.

05

Metrics & Reporting Setup

Identify the KPIs that actually matter for your business, then build simple dashboards and check-in rhythms so you always know where you stand, without spending hours in spreadsheets.

06

VIP Strategy Day

A focused, single-day intensive where we tackle your biggest operational challenge together. Walk away with a clear plan, custom deliverables, and a 30-day action checklist.
