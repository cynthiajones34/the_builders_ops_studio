The Architecture

One practice, two platforms.

The public site does the marketing; the portal does the coaching. A new client flows from one to the other without the coach touching a spreadsheet.

Part 01

The Marketing Site

yamswellness.com, a warm, editorial site that tells the brand story and moves visitors toward one action: applying for a Vibe Check call.

  • Home, Work With Me, About, Apply, Contact, and Terms pages
  • Four packages presented with clear pricing and inclusions
  • Free 5-Day Alignment Reset email opt-in
  • Vibe Check application form wired straight into the pipeline
Part 02

The Client Portal

A secure, login-protected space where each client's coaching journey lives, plus a private admin where the coach runs the entire practice.

  • Client dashboard, intake, session prep, and progress tracking
  • Shared resource library curated by the coach
  • Contract review and e-signing built in
  • Coach admin: leads, clients, calendar, contracts, financials, reporting

The Application Pipeline

From applicant to client, automatically.

Every application triggers a chain the coach never has to think about. The lead is captured, both sides get the right email, and enrolling a new client takes one click.

01

Application submitted

A visitor completes the Vibe Check application: goals, package interest, and what's bringing them in right now.

02

Emails go out instantly

The applicant receives a branded confirmation with a summary of what they shared; the coach gets a notification with the full application.

03

Lead lands in the admin

The application appears in the Leads screen, filterable by package, ready to review and schedule.

04

One click to enroll

When it's a fit, the coach creates the client's portal account directly from the admin, credentials and welcome included.

The Client Experience

A private space for the real work.

Every client logs into a portal that holds their entire coaching journey: the intake form they complete before the first session, prep prompts before each meeting, session notes and coach reflections afterward, and a progress journal both client and coach write in.

A curated resource library and a direct line to the coach round it out, so nothing lives in scattered emails or lost PDFs.

Contracts, Handled

Templates in, signed agreements out.

The coach writes her coaching agreement once as a reusable template with merge fields for the client's name, package, and date. Sending a contract to a new client takes seconds; the fields fill themselves.

The client reviews and signs right inside their portal, and the signed agreement is stored on their record. No printing, no scanning, no third-party e-sign subscription.

The Back Office

The whole practice on one screen.

The admin side is protected by coach-only authentication and gives her everything: a dashboard snapshot, the lead pipeline, every client's full record, a session calendar, contract templates, a financials log, a message inbox, and reporting.

Client data is locked down with per-user security rules: each client can only ever see her own records, while the coach sees everything.

Under the Hood

Secure by design, free to run.

Both applications run on Google Firebase: hosting, authentication, and a real-time database guarded by per-user security rules. Cloud functions handle the application pipeline, account creation, and every automated email. No monthly software subscriptions.
