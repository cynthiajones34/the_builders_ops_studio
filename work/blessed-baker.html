Case Study — Custom Ordering Platform

The Blessed Baker and Son.

A fully custom, live ordering platform for a micro-bakery in Tucker, Georgia. It handles two types of customer orders, automates weekly standing orders by text message, manages an email newsletter, and gives the owner a private dashboard to run everything — with no third-party apps and no monthly subscription.

The Customer Experience

Two clear ways to order.

First-time visitors land on a home page that explains exactly how ordering works, then choose the path that fits — weekly pickup or custom delivery. An FAQ, contact page, and terms page round out the customer-facing site, all mobile-friendly.

Path 01

Micro-Bakery Pickup

Weekly pickup orders, submitted over the weekend and collected on Fridays. Cupcakes by the dozen or half-dozen, and cake jars in two sizes across six signature flavors.

  • Tap-to-add menu with a live order summary
  • Real-time capacity tracker — customers see what's still available
  • Standing order toggle for automatic weekly orders
  • Instant email confirmation to customer and owner
Path 02

Custom Drop-Off Orders

Custom baked goods delivered to ten schools in the Lakeside High School cluster of DeKalb County — a delivery zone the owner manages herself from the admin panel.

  • School picker fed live from the admin settings
  • Minimum 2-day lead time enforced on the date picker
  • Free-form request field for custom cakes and special orders
  • Owner confirms details and arranges payment directly

Live Order Form

An order form that thinks ahead.

Customers tap items into a live order summary that totals as they go — no cart, no checkout maze. A capacity tracker shows how many dozen are still available this week, so the owner never over-commits her oven.

When she hits her limit, takes a vacation, or blocks a holiday week, the form automatically shows a closed message she writes herself. Pricing, capacity, and availability are all managed from the admin panel — no code changes, ever.

The Back Office

A private dashboard that runs the whole business.

Behind a password-protected admin page, the owner gets a complete operations center: a weekly snapshot, a full order calendar, filterable order tables with bulk status updates, customer order histories, and one-click CSV exports.

Reporting charts show best-selling flavors, busiest schools, and revenue trends. A built-in email tab sends the weekly menu drop to newsletter subscribers — with scheduling, recipient filtering, and live preview. Everything she can change lives in Settings: prices, capacity limits, school list, open/close toggles, and blocked vacation dates.

Calendar

The Signature Feature

Standing orders that run themselves.

Loyal customers check one box — "Make this a standing weekly order" — and from then on, their order processes automatically every Thursday at 9:00 AM. They manage everything by text message. The owner does nothing.

  • 01

    Thursday, 9:00 AM

    The system checks for blocked weeks, creates an order for every active standing customer, and texts each one a confirmation.

  • 02

    Customers reply by text

    SKIP pauses one week. STOP cancels for good. The system handles both automatically — no action needed from the owner.

  • 03

    Guaranteed weekly revenue

    The Standing Orders tab shows every active subscriber, their items, and a guaranteed weekly revenue figure the owner can plan around.

Under the Hood

Built to cost nothing to run.

The platform runs on Google Firebase — hosting, real-time database, and the scheduled cloud functions that process standing orders every Thursday. SendGrid delivers email, Twilio powers the two-way SMS, and the whole system runs with no monthly software subscription.
